Our History

Established in recent times by a group, great minds healthcare began as a humble clinic serving the rural community of Knust. Dr. Boateng, a visionary and dedicated physician, saw the need for accessible healthcare in the area and embarked on a mission to provide compassionate medical services to all.
Over the years, Great Minds HealthCare Center grew from its modest beginnings into a cornerstone of healthcare excellence in the region. Through the tireless efforts of Dr. Boateng and his team, the hospital expanded its facilities and services, catering to the evolving needs of the community.
Today, Maplewood General Hospital stands as a beacon of hope and healing, serving as a trusted healthcare partner for generations of families. From its humble origins to its present-day prominence, the hospital's journey is a testament to the power of perseverance, compassion, and community spirit in the pursuit of health and wellness.
As Great Minds HealthCare Center looks towards the future, it remains steadfast in its mission to provide exceptional healthcare services with integrity, compassion, and excellence, honoring its rich legacy while embracing the opportunities and challenges of tomorrow.
